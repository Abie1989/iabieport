About Me

As the Founder & CEO of Kint3D and Bluewave Dimension, I am a seasoned multidisciplinary digital artist with over 9+ years of expertise in 3D modeling, UI/UX design, and game development. My portfolio is a testament to my ability to merge artistic creativity with practical functionality, crafting everything from lifelike 3D environments to polished user interfaces and dynamic web solutions. My forte lies in transforming creative concepts into captivating digital experiences, utilizing the latest tools and design methodologies. My passion for digital creation shines through in my work, where I excel at integrating engaging 3D visuals with user-friendly interfaces and robust coding. I am dedicated to closing the gap between design and development, ensuring that digital products are not just envisioned but fully realized through responsive web and mobile applications.
